Cognito Bootstrap Vue

This site provides an example of how to use Amazon Cognito with Vue.js

To give it a try:

  1. Sign up using your email address, this will send through an activation code.
  2. Once you get the activation code enter it with your email address.
  3. Sign in with your credentials.
  4. Request a password reset to recover an account.
  5. Change your password.
